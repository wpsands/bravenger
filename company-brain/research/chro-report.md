# R-002: Natter CHRO Report

**Full title:** "Leading the GenAI Generation: The Evolving Role of the CHRO from Boardroom to Frontline"
**Authors:** Charlie Woodward (CEO, Natter) + John Reid-Dodick (CPO, AlphaSense)
**Date:** 2024
**Methodology:** 58 CHROs and global HR leaders in deep-dive conversations on Natter's platform. 163 1:1 participant conversations. 309 qualitative insights generated. 684 data points captured.
**Participant profile:** 50% have 20+ years HR experience, 80% C-suite or VP, 7.4 million+ global employees represented across 6 continents. For 2/3 of participants, more than 26% of employees are frontline workers.
**Citation key:** `[Source: research/chro-report.md]`

## Key Statistics

| Stat | Context |
|------|---------|
| Only 24% of leaders have currently deployed GenAI or are using it company-wide | Most orgs are early stage — massive TAM for change management listening |
| Among leaders who HAVE deployed, 100% are confident their frontline is ready to adopt GenAI | Confidence comes from doing the work |
| Among leaders still planning/piloting, only 49% are confident their frontline workforce is ready to adopt GenAI | Gap = opportunity for Natter |
| Almost 25% of leaders were unsure or very unsure if their frontline workers are ready for GenAI | Uncertainty signal |
| Less than 40% of participants agree they get enough real-time, insightful feedback from their frontline to help navigate change management | Direct pain point Natter solves |
| 700+ collaborative ideas were generated amongst CHROs and summarized instantly using Natter's technology | Platform proof — speed + scale |
| Unanimous agreement across participants that GenAI will change the frontline worker experience for the better over the next 10 years | Directional consensus |
| 58 CHROs, 163 1:1 conversations, 309 qualitative insights, 684 data points | Methodology proof point |
| 7.4 million+ global employees represented | Scale of participant organizations |
