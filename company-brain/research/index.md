## Report Details

### R-001: Deloitte Digital × Natter White Paper

- **Full title:** "AI can cut costs: But at what cost to the workforce experience?"
- **Authors:** Amelia Dunlop (CXO, Deloitte Digital) + Charlie Woodward (CEO, Natter)
- **Date:** 2024
- **Methodology:** 142 senior workforce leaders in a 90-minute roundtable on Natter's platform, plus 304 pre-roundtable interviews
- **Participant profile:** 36% Chief People Officers, 28% Employee Experience leadership, 78% C-Suite or Department Heads, global representation
- **Named participants:** Pfizer (Wendy Mayer, VP Candidate Experience), AlphaSense (John Reid-Dodick, CPO), Legal & General (Emmajane Varley), Square (Tracy Krilich, Head of EX), SAP (Lynne Krishna, Global Change Lead), AWS (Cassandra Babilya, EX Leader), Mambu (Vivienne Heffernan)
- **Citation key:** `[Source: research/R-001]`

### R-002: Natter CHRO Report

- **Full title:** "Leading the GenAI Generation: The Evolving Role of the CHRO from Boardroom to Frontline"
- **Authors:** Charlie Woodward (CEO, Natter) + John Reid-Dodick (CPO, AlphaSense)
- **Date:** 2024
- **Methodology:** 58 CHROs and global HR leaders in deep-dive conversations on Natter's platform. 163 1:1 participant conversations. 309 qualitative insights generated. 684 data points captured.
- **Participant profile:** 50% have 20+ years HR experience, 80% C-suite or VP, 7.4 million+ global employees represented across 6 continents. For 2/3 of participants, more than 26% of employees are frontline workers.
- **Named expert contributors:** John Reid-Dodick (CPO, AlphaSense), Samantha Hammock (EVP & CHRO, Verizon), Jacqui Canney (CPO, ServiceNow), Yaarit Silverstone (Senior MD, Accenture Strategy), Stephen Wroblewski (MD, Accenture Strategy), Marilyn Chaplin (CHRO, NTT), Christian Schmeichel (SVP, SAP), Soni Basi (Former Global CPO, Edelman), Josefin Erséus (CHRO Nordic, Teleperformance), Volker Schrank (VP HR & EX, Mondelez International)
- **Organization logos:** LVMH, Unilever, Hilton, State Farm, Diageo, PepsiCo, AlphaSense, U.S. Army, ServiceNow, Siemens, Coca-Cola, IKEA, Red Bull, ABB, SAP, CVS, GSK, Verizon, Mondelez, Edelman, ASML, NTT, Coty, Teleperformance, Marriott, U.S. Air Force, Burberry, Nestlé, Ubisoft, U.S. Olympic Committee, Morningstar, Sweco, Arizona State University, Builders FirstSource, GroupM

### R-003: Natter "AI & the Employee Voice" White Paper

- **Full title:** "AI & the Employee Voice: From Questionnaires to Conversations"
- **Authors:** Charlie Woodward (CEO), Dan Willard (CDO), Matthew Noble (Head of AI & Data)
- **Date:** Late 2024 / Early 2025
- **Methodology:** 240 semi-structured interviews (~50 min each) with People Listening leaders from 34 countries, 6 continents, 200 companies. Then 420 peer-to-peer conversations via Natter across same population in 40 minutes. 29,810 qualitative statements captured, 492,184 words transcribed, 96 themes and insights generated.
- **Named speakers:** Marc Solow (MD, Human Capital Analytics & Insights, Deloitte), Suzanne Love Beck (Talent Experience & Engagement Leader, Deloitte), David Meza (Head of People Analytics, NASA), Ann-Marie Johnson (Senior Director, People Strategy & Analytics, Salesforce), Bill Morrissey (Head of Employee Listening System & Products, Amazon/AWS), Neera Ridler-Mayor (Human Capital / Workforce Analytics & Insight, Deloitte)
- **Organization logos:** Bank of America, Salesforce, Expedia, PepsiCo, Nissan, Target, Dyson, Swarovski, Roche, Colgate-Palmolive, IKEA, NASA, General Mills, H&M, Bayer, Ford, LEGO, Adidas, BlackRock, AWS, Kraft Heinz, Royal Caribbean, GSK, EDF
