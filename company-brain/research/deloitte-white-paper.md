# R-001: Deloitte Digital x Natter White Paper

**Full title:** "AI can cut costs: But at what cost to the workforce experience?"
**Authors:** Amelia Dunlop (CXO, Deloitte Digital) + Charlie Woodward (CEO, Natter)
**Date:** 2024
**Methodology:** 142 senior workforce leaders in a 90-minute roundtable on Natter's platform, plus 304 pre-roundtable interviews
**Participant profile:** 36% Chief People Officers, 28% Employee Experience leadership, 78% C-Suite or Department Heads, global representation
**Citation key:** `[Source: research/deloitte-white-paper.md]`

## Key Statistics

| Stat | Context |
|------|---------|
| Only 10% of leaders currently use AI often for decisions, but 74% anticipate using AI often within 5 years | AI adoption acceleration |
| Employees view employers as 188% less empathetic and human when AI tools are offered (Deloitte TrustID 2023) | Trust deficit — the core problem Natter solves |
| Employees who highly trust their employers are 50% less likely to look for a new job and 180% more likely to feel motivated (Deloitte TrustID 2021-2022) | ROI of trust — business case for employee listening |
| 69% of 1:1 Natter conversations cited improved efficiency in routine tasks as the top hope for AI | What leaders want from AI |
| ~1/3 of conversations cited concern for bias and inclusion challenges of AI | Tension leaders face |
| 70% of conversations cited the need for education & transparency to drive trust with AI | Trust as prerequisite |
| 69% of leaders believe AI will improve employee creativity to some degree | Optimism signal |
| 42% of conversations cited concern for decline in quality of work | Fear signal |
| 58% of conversations discussed wanting to use AI to facilitate more effective feedback loops | Direct Natter value prop |
| 56% of conversations cited concern about ethical use of AI for data security and privacy | Privacy as top concern — Natter's ISO 27001 response |
| ~1/3 of conversations cited AI's ability to unlock time for meaningful human interactions | Time savings narrative |
| 41% of leaders believe AI will cause a decrease in quality of human connections | Tension — why conversations > surveys |
| Only 11% of 1,600+ global AI policies focused on regulating data privacy, cybersecurity, and IP (Deloitte Center for Government Insights 2023) | Regulatory gap — why proactive compliance matters |
| 142 senior workforce leaders participated in 90-minute roundtable | Platform proof point — scale demonstration |
