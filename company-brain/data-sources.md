# Data Sources

External data sources relevant to Bravenger's ICP (Series A/B B2B SaaS companies, 20-150 employees, AI-native or technical founder-led).

---

## Funding and Company Data

| Source | What It Provides | Use |
|--------|-----------------|-----|
| **Crunchbase** | Funding rounds, company stage, headcount, investors | ICP qualification. Identify Series A/B companies. Buying signal: recent funding. |
| **PitchBook** | Detailed funding data, valuation, cap table | Deeper funding intelligence for target accounts. |
| **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** | Headcount, hiring activity, title changes, company updates | Buying signals: new marketing hires, new PMM hires, team growth. |
| **BuiltWith / Wappalyzer** | Technology stack detection | Technographic qualification. Identify Markdown-friendly, AI-adopting teams. |
| **G2 / TrustRadius** | Software reviews, tech stack, buyer intent signals | Identify companies evaluating adjacent categories (CMS, DAM, content tools). |

## Hiring and Signal Data

| Source | What It Provides | Use |
|--------|-----------------|-----|
| **LinkedIn Jobs** | Open positions, job descriptions | Buying signals: hiring first marketer, first PMM, content roles with AI requirements. |
| **Glassdoor** | Company reviews, culture signals | Qualify company culture fit. Technical vs. non-technical orientation. |
| **GitHub** | Public repos, engineering activity, README quality | Technographic signal. Companies with strong GitHub presence are more likely Markdown-comfortable. |
| **Twitter/X** | Founder activity, product announcements, hiring tweets | Real-time buying signals. Founder frustration tweets. Product launch announcements. |

## Data Source Usage Rules

1. **External data supplements, not replaces, the Brain.** Data sources provide evidence and signals. The Brain holds the conclusions.
2. **Cite data sources when used.** If a claim in the Brain is based on external data, cite the source.
3. **Refresh cadence.** Company and hiring data: weekly. Market and industry data: monthly. Community signals: continuous.
4. **Privacy and compliance.** Use publicly available data only. Do not scrape private profiles or violate terms of service. LinkedIn data must be accessed through official tools (Sales Navigator, not scraping).
5. **Signal, not surveillance.** We monitor buying signals to have relevant conversations. We do not track individuals or build dossiers.
